Reasons for Creating Vertical Slides

This type of slide layout can be very helpful for keeping proper linearity in your presentation without cramming too much onto one slide. Some examples of what you can use vertical slides for would be:

    • Adding additional explanations for the subject matter
    • Making a side-note that feels relevant
    • Adding a list of references
    • Breaking up a lengthy explanation into digestible parts
